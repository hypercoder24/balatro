How Scoring Works
Chips × Mult = Your Score
Every hand has base Chips and Mult. Cards you play add chips. Jokers add chips, mult, or both.
+Mult adds to your multiplier. ×Mult MULTIPLIES it — always put ×Mult jokers on the RIGHT.
Example: 80 chips × 4 mult = 320. Add a ×3 joker on the right: 80 × 4 × 3 = 960!
You need to beat the Blind's chip requirement. Higher antes need exponentially bigger scores.
